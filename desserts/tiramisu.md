# Tiramisu recipe


## Ingredients

- 500g mascarpone cheese
- 4 eggs
- 100g sugar
- 200ml strong espresso, cooled
- 200g ladyfinger biscuits
- Cocoa powder for dusting


## Instructions

- Beat egg yolks with sugar until pale and creamy
- Mix in mascarpone until smooth
- In a separate bowl, whisk egg whites to stiff peaks
- Fold egg whites gently into the mascarpone mixture
- Dip ladyfingers briefly into the espresso and layer in a dish
- Spread half the mascarpone cream over the ladyfingers
- Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and remaining cream
- Dust generously with cocoa powder
- Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving
